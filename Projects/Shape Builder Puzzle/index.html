How To Play

  • Press and drag from a numbered cell to draw a shape.
  • The shape's area must equal the cell's number (e.g. 4 creates 2x2 or 1x4).
  • Drawn shapes must contain exactly one numbered cell.
  • Cover the entire grid completely with no overlaps or gaps.
  • Double-click a shape to remove/clear it from the board.
